Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast (~2.5 pounds), cut into chunks
  • 4 peppers and 2 Tbsp sauce from can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, (chop them up manually)
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 onion, dice
  • 6 cloves garlic diced
  • juice of 1 lime
  • 1 Tbsp cumin
  • 1 Tbsp beef boullion
  • 1 Tbsp oregano
  • 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 cup beef broth

Instructions

  1. throw all into crock pot
  2. cook on low 4 hours
