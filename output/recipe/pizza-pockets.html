Ingredients

  • turkey pepperoni
  • mozzarella cheese
  • flour tortillas
  • pizza sauce

Instructions

  1. Slice Pepperoni into shreds
  2. Shred mozzarella into a bowl
  3. Add basil and oregano to bowl
  4. Make cones with tortillas, add pepperoni/cheese mixture, seal shut
  5. spray with oil and cook in the air fryer at 375 for 10 minutes
  6. Serve with pizza sauce for dipping
