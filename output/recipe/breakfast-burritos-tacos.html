Sausage

Ingredients

  • breakfast sausage or chorizo or ground turkey/beef or bacon
  • eggs
  • bell pepper
  • 1/2 onion
  • potato

Instructions

  1. Break down sausage and fry. Remove from pan.
  2. Dice potatoes into small cubes and fry until golden, remove from pan
  3. Scramble eggs, remove from pan
  4. Dice and Saute veggies
