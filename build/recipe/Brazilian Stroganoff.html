Ingredients

  • Chicken (breasts or thighs)
  • Butter
  • Diced onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Heavy cream (or crema)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Mushrooms (optional)

Instructions

  1. Saute chicken, remove chicken from pan
  2. Saute diced onion then garlic
  3. Add tomato sauce, ketchup and cream, simmer
  4. Optionally add mushrooms
  5. Dice the cooked chicken, add back into pan
  6. season with salt and pepper
