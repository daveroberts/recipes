Healthier Cheesecake Dessert
Share Recipe 📧
Note: This recipe rocks!
https://thebigmansworld.com/healthy-cheesecake/ 🔗

Ingredients

  • pie crust
  • 1 block reduced fat cream cheese
  • 2 cups non fat light vanilla greek yogurt (Add vanilla if plain)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup sugar substitute
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mix, put into pie crust
  2. Bake at 325F for 65 minutes
  3. Remove and let it cool
  4. Place in fridge
