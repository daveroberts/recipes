Ingredients

  • 1 package brocolli
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 packet ranch seasoning

Instructions

  1. Microwave brocolli
  2. Add brocolli to food processor
  3. In a large bowl, mix brocolli, cheese, eggs, ranch
  4. Form into fries
  5. Air fry until golden brown
