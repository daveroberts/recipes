Lamb Pulao

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp Black Pepper Corns
  • 1 tbsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Seeds
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 3 Black Cardamom Pods
  • 5 Cardamom pods
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 2 small Onions
  • 1 whole head of Garlic
  • 3 lbs Lamb Shoulder
  • 1/2 cup canola oil
  • 1 tbsp Ginger Turmeric and Garlic Paste.
  • 3 cups of washed Basmati rice

Instructions

  1. Preparation Begin by making a stock. Grab a large pot on low-medium heat and add black pepper corns, cumin and coriander seeds, cinnamon sticks, cardamom pods, and cloves. After a few minutes the spices should be toasted and fragrant.
  2. Add in 1 whole onion, 1 whole garlic, lamb and salt, submerge everything so it's just covered with water. Cover it on medium heat and cook for 1 hour.
  3. Taste the broth for seasoning. Remove the meat, onions, and garlic and strain the stock.
  4. In the same pot on medium heat, add back the garlic and onion that you previously used, along with one freshly slices onion, ginger/turmeric/garlic paste, and some oil. Mix for a couple of minutes.
  5. Bring your lamb back into the pot, and get a bit of colour on your lamb. Add your lamb stock. Bring everything up to a boil and then add 3 cups of washed basmati rice and mix.
  6. Cook on medium-high heat until the broth is bubbling just below the rice. Cover it and let this steam on low-medium heat for about 10 minutes. Plate and garnish with raita yogurt.
