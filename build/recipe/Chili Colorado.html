Chili Colorado Slow Cooker

Note: official recipe calls for 4 cups stock, 3 was plenty. Mine was undercooked but I cut larger chunks

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chuck roast
  • 3 ancho, 3 guajillo chilies
  • 5-10 chile de arbol (optional)
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 1 onion
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon oregano

Instructions

  1. deseed chilies. toast and simmer with a cup of beef stock for 20 minutes or more. Blend well
  2. cut chuck roast into small strips. Sear.
  3. Put diced onion into pan, deglaze with beef stock
  4. Add all to slow cooker. 2 hours on low. Uncover, 2 more hours to thicken sauce and finish
