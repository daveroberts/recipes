Burger Stew Cold Weather

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • onion, diced
  • carrots, diced
  • celery, diced
  • seasoning: basil, salt, pepper
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • 4 cups potatoes, diced
  • milk
  • 1 pound melting cheese (velveeta or other)
  • sour cream or cream cheese
  • bacon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook beef and diced onion until crispy, remove and set aside
  2. Dice / mince carrots and celery, add seasoning (basil, salt, pepper), saute in oil and butter until soft
  3. Add 1/4 cup flour, cook for a couple minutes
  4. Add chicken stock and potatoes, simmer until potatoes are tender
  5. Add milk, cheese, and a few large spoonfuls of sour cream, simmer until cheese is melted
  6. Add ground beef and crumbled bacon
