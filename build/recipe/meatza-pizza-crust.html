Meatza Pizza Crust
Share Recipe 📧
Note: This was a banger!
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qmhAuXfTRVg 🔗

Ingredients

  • can of chicken
  • 1 egg
  • half cup paresan cheese (powdered)
  • pizza spices (dried basil, oregano, etc)

Instructions

  1. Mix it all together with a mixer
  2. spread onto parchment paper
  3. Bake at 450F for about 20 minutes
  4. Add toppings, broil for two minutes
