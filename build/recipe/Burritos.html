Meat

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast

Instructions

  1. Can use any meat, but chicken breast is good. sous vide it then dice it small

Spice blend

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp paprika (smoked preferred)
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander
  • 1/2 Tbsp cumin
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Ingredients

  • cilantro
  • green pepper
  • jalapeno
  • 1 can beans (pinto preferred)
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • lime juice
  • cream cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • tortillas

Instructions

  1. Dice peppers and jalapenos
  2. mince cilantro, add lime juice and salt
  3. mostly drain a can of beans, add beans, peppers and jalapenos to skillet
  4. Add tomato sauce and spices, add lime juice and salt if needed
  5. Add diced chicken and cheese
  6. Put into burritos
