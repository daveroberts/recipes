Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate: 3.5oz chopped
  • Sugar: 2 Tbsp
  • Cocoa Powder: 1 Tbsp
  • Cornstarch: 1 teaspoon
  • Milk: 1 cup

Instructions

  1. Add dark chocolate, sugar, cocoa powder and splash of milk to a small saucepan on medium heat. Stir until melted.
  2. Add in a cup of milk.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix cornstarch and splash of milk to make a slurry. Add slurry to melted chocolate slowly while stirring. Stir and heat until thickened.
