Meat filling

Ingredients

  • ground beef (can be vegan)
  • taco seasoning
  • onion (optional)
  • mushrooms (optional)
  • beans (black/pinto) (optional)
  • red lentils (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brown off meat, add in other ingredients

Base Layer

Ingredients

  • large flour tortillas
  • shredded mexican cheese

Instructions

  1. Add 7 tortillas to pan
  2. Add meat layer, add cheese layer

Second layer

Ingredients

  • rotel
  • corn
  • tortilla chips
  • lettuce
  • sour cream

Instructions

  1. Add in next layer. All ingredients optional

Bake

Instructions

  1. Place another baking sheet on top, Into the oven at 420F for 20 minutes
  2. serve with taco sauce
