Ingredients

  • Ground Pork (or beef); Lazy version, roast beef
  • lettuce (baby gem, napa)
  • Carrots (shredded)
  • Sesame oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Soy Sauce
  • brown sugar
  • lime juice
  • cilantro
  • spring onions

Instructions

  1. Cook ground pork/beef; or slice and add roast beef to fry up
  2. Add other ingredients
  3. Add to lettuce wraps
