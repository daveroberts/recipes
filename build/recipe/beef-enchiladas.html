Beef Enchiladas Cold Weather

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • tortillas
  • onion
  • shredded cheddar/mexican cheese
  • can beans
  • can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans red enchilada sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook meat and onions
  2. Combine all in stand mixer
  3. Add to tortillas, add to baking dish, 350F for 40min
