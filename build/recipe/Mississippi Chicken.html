Mississippi Chicken
https://www.plainchicken.com/oven-baked-mississippi-chicken/ 🔗

Ingredients

  • Four pieces chicken (breast or any)
  • 1 packet ranch dressing
  • 1 package au jus gravy mix
  • 1/3 cup pepperoncini peppers or banana peppers
  • 1/2 cup butter, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425. Spray 9x13 pan
  2. Place chicken in dish, sprinkle with ranch seasoning and au jus
  3. top with peppers and butter
  4. bake for 25-30 minutes or until chicken is 165 internal
