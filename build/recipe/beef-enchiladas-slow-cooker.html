Ingredients

  • onion
  • lean ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • black beans
  • rotel
  • sweet corn
  • mexican cheese
  • enchilada sauce
  • corn tortillas
  • light sour cream

Instructions

  1. In a pan, fry up diced onion, ground beef and spices
  2. Add drained black beans, drained rotel, drained corn, cheese, and half of enchilada sauce to a separate bowl
  3. Add beef and onions to bowl, mix it up
  4. One option is to layer the slow cooker with corn tortillas and the mix. Top with cheese
  5. Another option is to forget the slow cooker and roll them up, placing onto a baking sheet and putting them in the oven.
