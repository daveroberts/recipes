Main Dish

Ingredients

  • quick oats
  • grated apple or applesauce
  • yogurt (plain or vanilla)
  • cinnamon

Instructions

  1. mix quick oats, yogurt, cinnamon and either apple sauce or grated apple in a bowl, let sit for 10 minutes

(Optional) Toppings

Ingredients

  • quick oats
  • almonds
  • honey or maple syrup
  • diced apple

Instructions

  1. Toast almonds, oats and some diced apples
  2. Top bowls with toasted mix and honey or maple sytrup
