Ingredients

  • high protein pasta (1 box)
  • ground beef, 1 pound
  • zucchini
  • onion
  • mushrooms
  • cottage cheese (blended)
  • pasta sauce, 1 jar
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. cook and drain pasta
  2. dice onion, zucchini, mushrooms, cook with ground beef
  3. blend cottage cheese
  4. Add all to a bowl and mix
  5. Add mixture to a baking dish and bake at 350F for 45 minutes
  6. top with some cheese, melt, serve
