Air Fryer Pulled Chicken Air Fryer
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NCP6VPf505A üîó

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • half stick of butter
  • honey
  • bbq sauce

Instructions

  1. Air fry chicken breasts at 390 for 20 minutes, remove and shread with forks
  2. In a pan, mix a half stick of butter, some honey and enough bbq sauce for the chicken, mix with shredded chicken in a bowl
  3. Put back in the air fryer at 390 for 7 minutes
