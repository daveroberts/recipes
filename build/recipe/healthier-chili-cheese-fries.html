Ingredients

  • Frozen Fries
  • ground meat
  • Red pepper
  • onion
  • 80g low fat cottage cheese
  • 30g low fat cheddar or mozzarella
  • 30g low fat milk
  • salt, pepper, garlic, paprika, cayenne

Instructions

  1. Make fries in air fryer
  2. Cook up beef and onions, add red pepper at end
  3. In a blender, add cheeses and spices,blend until smooth
  4. Add beef then cheese mixture to fries
