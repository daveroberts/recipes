Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • chipotle garlic seasoning (or any)
  • low fat cheese (feta, mozarella)
  • low carb tortilla

Instructions

  1. Add oil spray to a small pan and add two eggs
  2. Add seasoning to top, break the yolks and flip
  3. Add cheese
  4. Remove from pan and add to a tortilla
  5. Add ketchup, roll, and fry seal closed
