Brazillian Lemonade Dessert
Share Recipe 📧
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2hF_w7X4fEY 🔗

Ingredients

  • 3 limes
  • sweetened condensed milk

Instructions

  1. wash and half three limes, add to blender with water
  2. puree and strain out liquid
  3. Add liquid, sweetened condensed milk and ice to blender
  4. blend and enjoy
