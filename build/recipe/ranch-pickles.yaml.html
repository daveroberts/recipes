Ingredients

  • jar of pickle spears
  • ranch seasoning packet
  • bacon
  • light ranch dressing and/or greek yogurt for dipping

Instructions

  1. Add ranch seasoning to jar of pickles, let it sit for a day
  2. Wrap pickles in bacon, air fry at 350F for 9 minutes
