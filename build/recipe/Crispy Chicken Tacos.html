Ingredients

Instructions

Consomme

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 3 guajillo chilles
  • 2 ancho chilles
  • 10 chile de arbol
  • chicken broth
  • bay leaf
  • oregano, cumin

Instructions

  1. toast the chilles, add the rest of the ingredients, let soften
  2. blend, strain back into liquid
