Homemade English Muffins Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1.25 cup water
  • 3 Tbsp neutral oil
  • yeast - 3/4 tsp instant yeast or 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp sea salt
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • cornmeal

Instructions

  1. combine yogurt, water, oil, yeast, flour, salt and sugar in a large bowl, stir into a dough
  2. Let sit out for 30 minutes, stretch and fold (very light mixing). repeat 2 more times, let sit out for one hour, put in fridge for 10 hours (up to 48 hours)
  3. Line a baking pan with cornmeal
  4. Roll into a log and cut dough into 12 pieces. Form 12 balls, put onto baking pan. Add cornmeal to tops of balls, cover with plastic wrap, let proof for one or two hours
  5. cook in a skillet, slap them down with a spatula, cover with a lid to capture steam
