Onion Pasta
Share Recipe 📧
Note: Great for leftover extra onions
https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/11868/onion-pasta/ 🔗

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 5 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon chicken bouillon
  • 1 pound uncooked pasta, cooked according to package directions

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Cook onions in a separate bowl
  3. Add basic, black pepper, water and bouillon. Simmer 10 minutes
  4. Combine pasta and onion mixture. Sprinkle with cheese.
