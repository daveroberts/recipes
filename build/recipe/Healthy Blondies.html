Ingredients

  • Natural smooth peanut butter, amount varies between 2 1/2 cups or one 16oz jar
  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained
  • 5 tablespoons maple syrup
  • dark chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix PB, chickpeats and maple syrup in a food processor
  2. fold in dark chocolate chips
  3. Add to baking pan, bake at 350 for 25 minutes
