Garlicky Smashed Cucumbers Side Dish
Note: Good for leftover cucumbers

Ingredients

  • 6 cucumbers
  • 2 Tbsp spicy chili oil
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Cut cucumber into pieces. Smash them up to help them absorb other incredients.
  2. Mix it all together. Season with salt
