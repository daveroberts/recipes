Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs or breasts
  • 1 onion, diced
  • ginger garlic paste
  • 1 28oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup coconut milk or 3 Tbsp greek yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • salt and pepper
  • cilantro for garnish
  • rice or naan as side

Instructions

  1. saute onion, spices, transfer to slow cooker
  2. Add allexpect coconut milk and/or yogurt to slow cooker
  3. cook for 6 hours on low
  4. Remove chicken and shred, re-add to slow cooker with coconut milk
  5. Finish cooking uncovered to thicken, taste for additional salt
