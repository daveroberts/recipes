Beans and Rice Cheap
Share Recipe 📧
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SmmCgGLvdlQ 🔗

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dried beans (pinto, lima)
  • 4 cups broth (or water + better than bouillon
  • 1/2 cup evoo
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. soak beans the night before in water with 1 tsp baking soda
  2. heat oil in instant pot, add minced garlic then add drained beans and everything else
  3. Cook on high pressure for 45 minutes. Takes about 1 hour
  4. Take out two cups of beans and blend them with an immersion blender
