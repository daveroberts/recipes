Faux Wendy's Spicy Chicken Sandwich
Note: This is great, but deep frying is kind of a pain. Maybe half deep fry? Added pickeled jalapenos because we had them and they were awesome
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SDAHsZGJ4AI üîó

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Hot Sauce
  • egg
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • mayo
  • flour, cayenne, paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, salt, vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Pound Chicken breasts flat
  2. In a bowl, whisk an egg and hot sauce.
  3. In a second bowl, add flour and cayenne, paprika, garlic powder, black pepper and salt.
  4. Dredge chicken through dry bowl, then wet bowl, then dry again. Freeze chicken if possible.
  5. In a small skillet, heat vegetable oil on medium. Use enough vegetable oil to almost submerge breasts. Fry for 3-4 minutes per side. After pulling out, add salt to fried chicken
  6. Serve on toasted buns with mayo, lettuce and tomato
