Red and Green Salsa Simple Side Dish
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Hpd_p7LRk_k üîó

Ingredients

  • 3-5 red tomatoes (cheap is fine, roma)
  • 6 tomatillos
  • 4, 10, or 20 chile de arbol (mild, medium, hot)
  • pepper (habanero preferred, jalapeno, bell, poblano, serrano, etc works too)
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic
  • cilantro
  • 1/2 onion

Instructions

  1. Roast red tomatoes, garlic and onion in pan under broiler, burn them
  2. Boil tomatillos, all chile de arbol, habaneo, drain once water boiling
  3. Put roasted tomatoes into bowl, add half cup water
  4. Green salsa: blend tomatillos, habaneo and half chile de arbol, half garlic, 20 sprigs cilantro, 1/4 onion and salt
  5. Red salsa: Blend red tomatoes, half chile de arbol, half clove garlic, 1/4 onion, oregano
