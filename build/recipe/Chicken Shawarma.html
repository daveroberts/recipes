Spice Marinade

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp all spice
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp onion
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp clove
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp msg
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1.5tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1.5 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 Tbsp white vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • liquid smoke (optional, or use real smoke, see below)

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add olive oil, lemon juice and tomato paste
  3. optionally, add lemon zest

Chicken Prep

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs

Instructions

  1. Slice thighs into half inch pieces
  2. Marinade overnight

Toum sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 full bulb of garlic
  • 1 cup neutral oil (corn, canola, vegetable, avocado, grapeseed, sunflower, peanut)
  • 4 Tbsp lemon juice
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients together

Make fries (optional)

Ingredients

  • frozen fries

Instructions

  1. Make some frensh friend in the air frier. Goes well in/with Shawarma

Cooking the chicken

Ingredients

  • dill relish or sliced pickes

Instructions

  1. Slice some dill pickles if you don't have dill relish
  2. Sear the chicken on high heat. Don't crowd the pan. Allow a crust to develop

Smoke the chicken (optional)

Ingredients

  • Pan with cover
  • Charcoal from indian / middle eastern

Instructions

  1. In a pan which has a cover, move the chicken to the edges to clear a cirlce in the center of the pan
  2. Line a very small ramakin with aluminium foil. Add neutral oil to the ramakin, put the ramakin in the center of the pan
  3. Move the pan outside
  4. Light a small charcoal and put it into the oil. The oil will burn and make smoke. Cover your pan. Let the meat smoke for 5-10 minutes

Final Assembly

Ingredients

  • dill relish or sliced pickes
  • pomegranite molassses
  • french fries
  • pitas
  • parchment paper
  • aluminum foil

Instructions

  1. Add pita to pan to soak leftover chicken fat, spread toum sauce on pita in pan, add chicken and sliced dill pickles
  2. Add fries (optional)
  3. Add pomegranite molassses (optional)
  4. Wrap pita in parchment paper
  5. Wrap pita in aluminum foil, heat in pan, slice diagonally
  6. Serve with Toum
