Spinach Curry to revise Indian

Ingredients

  • 2 bags of spinach
  • fresh chilis
  • ghee, corriander seeds, cardamon pods, cumin seeds, garam masala
  • ginger, garlic
  • onion
  • lentils (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee, add whole corriander seeds, cardamon pods, cumin seeds
  2. Add fresh chilis, onion and salt. Cook down
  3. Add ginger garlic paste
  4. Add two bags of spinach, add some water and simmer
  5. Add salt and garam masala
  6. (Optional) Add channa dal or lentils. Add more water
  7. Let it cook for 30 minutes
