Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • 1 cup lentils
  • onion, diced
  • red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can of corn, drained
  • 14oz can green chilies
  • 1/2 packet taco seasoning
  • sour cream
  • lime juice
  • shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook lentils
  2. Combine lentils, onion, bell pepper, corn, chilies and taco seasoning in a pan
  3. Cook for a bit, top with cheese
  4. Combine sour cream, lime juice and water to make a smooth creamy topping
