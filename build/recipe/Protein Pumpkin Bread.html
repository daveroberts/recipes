Wet Ingrediets

Ingredients

  • 2 (4) eggs, mix really well
  • 250g (500g) pumpkin puree
  • 85g (170g) greek yogurt
  • small amount vanilla extract

Dry Ingrediets

Ingredients

  • 75g (150g) flour
  • 1 tsp (2 tsp) baking soda
  • 1 tsp (2 tsp) baking powder
  • 1 tsp (2 tsp) salt
  • 45g (90g) sugar substitute (Erythritol or monk fruit or other)
  • 2 (4) packets sweetener (Stevia, Spenda, etc)
  • 50g (100g) whey protein powder (vanilla preferred)
  • 1 tsp (2) pumpkin pie spice

Instructions

  1. Add wet and dry ingredients to separate bowls
  2. Mix wet and try together
  3. Pour half of mixture into a separate bowl
  4. In one bowl, add the following:
  5. 12g (24) cocoa powder
  6. cinnamon
  7. cayenne
  8. 2 (4) packets sweetener (Stevia, Splenda, etc)
  9. Alternate pouring the mixtures into 1 (2) loaf pans
  10. Optionally, cut and swirl with a knife in the loaf pan
  11. Bake at 275 for 45 minutes
