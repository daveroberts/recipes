Spice Blend

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp Chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin

Marinade

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 bundle cilantro (reserve 1/4 for next day)
  • 1 tsp fresh rosemary
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tsp fresh sage
  • half of the spice blend
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • lemon juice
  • 5 pounts chicken thighs and drums

Instructions

  1. To a blender add lemon zest and its juice
  2. garlic
  3. shallot
  4. cilantro
  5. rosemary
  6. thyme
  7. sage
  8. spice blend
  9. olive oil
  10. blend it up
  11. Marinade chicken overnight

the next day

Ingredients

  • 3-4 large potatoes
  • Sheet pan
  • aluminum foil

Instructions

  1. Slice the potatoes into chunks, line a sheet tray, add olive oil and some of remaining spice blend
  2. Add chicken and marinade on top of potatoes
  3. Add remaining spice blend on top
  4. Cover with aluminum foil
  5. Bake at 375 for 40 minutes, remove foil, put back into oven
  6. Add minced garlic to a pan, cook for a minute, then add lemon juice and chopped cilantro, add this mixture to the top of your chicken
