Chicken Tikka Masala Indian

Ingredients

  • whole milk yogurt
  • 2 pounds chicken thighs, boneless skinless
  • basmati rice, cardamon pods
  • 28oz can tomatoes
  • heavy cream - 1 1/4 cup
  • cilantro (garnish)
  • 2 chilis
  • corriander seeds, cumin seeds, garlic, ginger, garam masala, turmeric, butter, paprika, kashmiri chili powder
  • 1 onion

Curry Paste

Instructions

  1. Toast in a dry pan: 1 Tbsp corriander seeds, 1/2 tsp cumin seeds for a couple minutes, remove from heat and add to bowl. Add to a blender and break apart the spices.
  2. Add to blender 7 cloves garlic, 2" knob ginger, 1 Tbsp garam masala, 1 tsp turmeric, 2 chilis de-stemmed, 1/4 cup water. blend it until smooth
  3. Add to blender 2 Tbsp canola oil, keep blending until smooth.

Chicken Marinade

Instructions

  1. Add to a bowl 1 cup plain whole milk yogurt, two Tbsp curry paste, 2tsp kosher salt, whisk together.
  2. Cut 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs into bite sized pieces. Add to bowl with marinade, cover and put in fridge for 30 minutes to overnight.

Rice

Instructions

  1. In pressure cooker, 1 to 1 ratio of washed basmati rice and water with some cardamon pods. Cook 6 minutes, natural release about 8 minutes.

Curry

Instructions

  1. Blend 28 oz can whole tomatoes, set aside
  2. In saute pan, heat oil and seer chicken on all sides. Remove chicken from pan and set aside.
  3. Reduce heat in pan and add 4 Tbsp butter. Add one diced onion. Cook five minutes
  4. Add all remaining curry paste, add 1 Tbsp paprika, 1 tsp kashmiri chili powder, heat another minute
  5. Add pureed tomatoes. Simmer 10 minutes.
  6. (Optional). Wait to cool and blend mixture for smoother texture.
  7. Add 1 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream, mix. Re-add chicken. Simmer 10 more minutes, serve over rice. Garnish with cilantro
