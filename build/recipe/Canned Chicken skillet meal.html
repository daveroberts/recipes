Ingredients

  • rice or noodles
  • 2 cans chicken breast
  • Tomato
  • Zuchini
  • bouillon

Instructions

  1. fry up chicken, tomato and zuchini in pan (with lid if possible) with oil or button
  2. Add water, bouillon and moodles or rice
  3. close lid, let cook
  4. season if needed
