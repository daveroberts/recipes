Chili Beef or Pork Lettuce Wraps Thai
Note: This is awesome! Maybe a little too intense, don't go crazy on brown sugar.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UzFoThs2Qpw üîó

Ingredients

  • lettuce (baby gem, napa)
  • Ground Pork (can be half pork and half beef)
  • Chili
  • fish sauce
  • lime
  • cilantro
  • spring onions

Instructions

  1. Season ground meat. Add oil to very hot pan and fry meat dark brown and crispy. Break up with spoon.
  2. Remove and drain off oil through sive. Wipe out pan.
  3. Add 1tsp sesame oil, chili, ginger, garlic to pan.
  4. Add brown sugar, add back in meat. Add fish sauce.
  5. Add zest of lime and lime juice. Add spring onions.
  6. Dipping sauce - 1tsp brown sugar, soy sauce, 1Tbsp sesame oil, 1Tbsp olive oil, 1tsp fish sauce, lime juice
  7. Add diced chili (keep seeds in). Add diced cilantro
