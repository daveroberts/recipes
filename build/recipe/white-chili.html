White Chili Slow Cooker Cold Weather
Share Recipe 📧
https://natashaskitchen.com/chicken-cream-cheese-chili/ 🔗

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts or 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 can white beans, drained
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 can corn with liquid
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with chilies
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1/2 bag ranch dressing mix (or whole bag)
  • half package cream cheese

Instructions

  1. Add all to slow cooker, in order
  2. If using breasts, cook for five hours then removed breasts to shred. Don't re-add until end to prevent drying
  3. slow cook the rest for 8 hours
  4. Service with cheddar cheese and corn bread
