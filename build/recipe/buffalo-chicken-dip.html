Buffalo Chicken Dip Side Dish
Share Recipe 📧
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uNvQ-YIOaKk 🔗

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chicken (12.5oz can)
  • cream cheese block
  • hot sauce (overpowering, go easy)
  • ranch seasoning packet
  • shredded cheese (mozzarella or other)
  • red peppers (for dipping)

Instructions

  1. throw ingredients into crock pot / instant pot
