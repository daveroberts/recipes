Ingredients

  • 1 pack of slider buns
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • shredded parmesan cheese
  • chicken thighs

Instructions

  1. Slice lettuce, add to bowl with dressing and cheese
  2. Saute or grill chicken
  3. Add to buns
