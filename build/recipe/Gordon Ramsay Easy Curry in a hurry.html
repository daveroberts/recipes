Gordon Ramsay Easy Curry in a hurry Indian
Share Recipe üìß

Ingredients

  • red onion
  • butternut squash, grated
  • ginger (or ginger garlic paste)
  • 2 fresh chilis
  • chicken (breast or thighs), diced into bite sized pieces
  • frozen peas
  • coconut milk
  • canned tomatoes
  • broth (few tablespoons)
  • curry powder or garam masala, 1 Tbsp
  • Baby spinach
  • cilantro (garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a non-stick pan, add oil and grated butternut squash, salt and curry powder.
  2. Grate the red onion, add to pan, season with more salt and stir
  3. Dice chilis, add to pan.
  4. Microplane grate ginger into pan. (or use paste), stir
  5. Create hole in center of pan, add raw chicken, season with salt, mix into pan
  6. Add peas, add chopped tomatoes, stir.
  7. Add coconut milk, mix
  8. Add broth to loosen curry (water is OK too). Taste for seasoning. Let cook for about 6 minutes to let chicken cook.
  9. Add spinach last minute

Roti bread

Instructions

  1. Mix 1/2 cup flour, pinch of salt, glug of olive oil, 1/2 cup water in a bowl into a dough bowl. Let rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Cut into four parts, roll each part into flatbread. In a clean non-stick pan, add pinch of salt, splash of oil, add flatbread, let it bubble up and turn over.
  3. Slice bread into pieces for dipping.
