Ingredients

  • 1 can cream of chicken
  • 1 can cream of mushroom
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • mushrooms (optional)
  • black pepper
  • rice (not in crock pot)
  • 1 can green beans (not in crock pot)

Instructions

  1. dice onion
  2. Add all to crock pot, 6 hours on low
  3. serve with rice and green beans
