Ingredients

  • 2-3 bananas
  • 200g oats
  • Protein powder (whey and cassein)
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • Sweetener
  • Milk (almond or other)
  • Topping: frozen blueberries
  • Toggping: Yogurt

Instructions

  1. Add ingredients and mix. Use enough sweetener so it's about the consistency of oatmeal
  2. Add toppings
  3. Put in fridge
  4. 350F for 35 minutes
