Ingredients

  • 12oz macaroni noodles (bowties preferred)
  • 8oz sour cream
  • 1 can cream of mushroom
  • Brown gravy mix packet
  • 1 pound ground turkey/beef

Instructions

  1. start macaroni
  2. brown meat, season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder
  3. If using fatty meat, drain excess oil
  4. In a bowl, mix brown gravy mix and milk/water, add to skillet
  5. Add sour cream to skillet
  6. Add cream of mushroom soup to skillet
  7. drain and add pasta, season and serve
