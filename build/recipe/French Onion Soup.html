French Onion Soup Slow Cooker to try

Overnight in the slow cooker, caramelize the onions

Ingredients

  • 6 yellow onions (more is great too)
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 Tbsp thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 stick butter

Instructions

  1. Slice onions, add everything to pot and cook overnight on low.
  2. If you're around, you can take off the lid to release steam to aid carmelization. Just set a reminder to check and stir if you do this

The next day in the slow cooker

Ingredients

  • 4 cans beef stock
  • demi glaze (optional)
  • 2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Tbsp flour (optional)
  • 1/2 cup alcohol (dry sherry, red wine or dark beer)

Instructions

  1. Add ingredients to slow cooker, let it cook on low until lunch or dinner

Serving

Ingredients

  • shredded cheese
  • toast

Instructions

  1. Serve with shredded cheese on top. Toast is a good side dish
