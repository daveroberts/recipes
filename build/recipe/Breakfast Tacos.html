Homemade flour tortilla

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • fat - 1/2 cup, lard, shortening or bacon fat

Instructions

  1. combine flour, salt and paking powder in bowl. Whisk together
  2. Add bacon fat and combine into crumbly texture
  3. Add 2/3 cup warm water while stirring to make dough ball. Leave in bowl, cover with plastic wrap, let it rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide dough into 12-15 portions. Preheat cast iron pan to medium high heat.
  5. Roll out tortillas thin and put into dry cast iron pan. Once bubbled, flip. About 30 seconds per side. Put tortillas into dry warm kitchen towell

Taco 1 - Bacon egg, and cheese

Instructions

  1. Bacon - prepare either in cast iron pan or on baking sheet. Line baking sheet with foil to collect fat. Toss into cold oven set to 425F, let it sit for 20-30 minutes or until crispy.
  2. Scrambled eggs - Add enough butter to coat bottom of pan and melt. Add eggs to a bowl and season with salt and pepper. Add eggs to hot buttered pan. Start stirring immediately. Stop at soft scramble.
  3. Add eggs, shredded cheese and bacon to tortilla

Taco 2 - Potato, egg, and cheese

Instructions

  1. Grate potato into hash. Wash potato hash to remove starch. Place potatoes into towell and ring out as much water as possible. Season heavily with salt and pepper in bowl.
  2. Heat non-stick skillet, coat with plenty of oil and butter and press potatoes into pan. Cook until crispy (3-5 minutes), flip, cook other side. Add salt on top.
  3. Add egg to tortilla, cheese and potatoes

Taco 3 - Chorizo, egg, cheese, avocado, diced sweet or red onion, cilantro

Instructions

  1. Remove chorizo from sausage skin. cook until crispy.
  2. In separate non-stick, add oil and/or butter until hot. Fry one egg per taco
  3. Add egg to taco, then chorizo, cheese, sliced avocado, onion and cilantro

Taco 4 - Vegetarian Taco - onion, garlic, serrano pepper, avocado, grated cheese, cilantro

Instructions
