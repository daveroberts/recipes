Lettuce Wraps Lunch Thai

Ingredients

  • Roast Beef lunch meat slices
  • Lettuce
  • Carrots (shredded)
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Soy Sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pan and fry/steam carrots to soften.
  2. Shred up roast beef slices, add to carrots, continue to fry to soften the beef.
  3. Add soy sauce and sesame seeds.
  4. Add to lettuce wraps
