Hummus Side Dish
Share Recipe üìß

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 Tablespoon Tahini
  • 1 Tablespoon Lemon or Lime Juice
  • Two pinches of salt
  • Garlic Powder (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil (optional)
  • Cumin (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Optional: Drain the chickpeas. I don't do this normally
  2. Put into blender and blend, taste and add salt
  3. (Optional) Top with paprika and olive oil
