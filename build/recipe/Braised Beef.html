Braised Beef Cold Weather

Ingredients

  • Tough meat cut: beef or lamb
  • carrots, onions, celery
  • aromatics: bay leaves, orange peels

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Bring meat up to room temperature, season with salt and pepper, fry in a dutch oven to get it charred on all sides
  3. Remove meat, add carrots, onions and celery. Let cook until carmelized
  4. Add red wine to pot to deglaze. Let alcohol cook off.
  5. Add water halfway up the pot and bring to a boil
  6. Lower to a simmer, add aromatics and cover. Move to oven. Cook for 1-2 hours depending on how much meat you have. Pull out when tender.
