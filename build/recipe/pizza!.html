Dough

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bread flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry yeast
  • glug of olive oil (~2 teaspoons)
  • garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions

  1. mix ingredients
  2. Form into a ball, put back in bowl, let it rise on the counter for an hour or two (or let rise in the fridge overnight)
  3. Put some olive oil into a nonstick pan. Add oregano, basil, salt, chili flakes, coat the bottom of pan
  4. Place dough onto pan, stretch into to edges. Rub olive oil on top. Let it rise in the pan for ~30 minutes
