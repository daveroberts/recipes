Mexican Hot Chocolate Dessert Cold Weather

8 servings

Ingredients

  • 6 12 oz cans evaporated milk
  • 4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • Pinch cayenne (optional heat)
  • 2 12oz bags semisweet Hershey’s chocolate chips
  • cinnamon stick (garnish)
  • Cocoa powder (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Stovetop: Combine into large pot, heat and stir until chocolate chips are melted, turn heat to low.
  2. Slow cooker: combine and cook on low for 2-3 hours, stir every 15-20 minutes
  3. Serve with dusting of cocoa powder and cinnamon stick
