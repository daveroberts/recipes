Spicy Chili Oil
Share Recipe üìß
Note: Good to use up leftover chicken fat

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chicken fat
  • 3 cloves garlic, 1" piece ginger
  • minced fresh chili
  • 1 Tbsp sesame seeds
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Heat up chicken fat until completely liquified.
  2. In another bowl, add other ingredients, slowly pour liquid chicken into bowl. Whisk together.
