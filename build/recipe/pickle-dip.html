Ingredients

  • 1 jar dill pickles
  • 2 cups plain greek yogurt
  • 8oz tub whipped cream cheese
  • bacon bits (whole container)
  • handfull shredded cheese (cheddar, mexican, whatever)
  • ranch seasoning packet (half packet for milder dip)
  • (optional) diced green onions
  • chips for (kettle, cape code, ridged, deep river)

Instructions

  1. Remove pickles from jar, chop in a food processor. Save some brine to thin out the
  2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well
  3. Add some brine to thin out dip if desired
  4. Serve with chips
