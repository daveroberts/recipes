marinade chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp paprika

  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • lime juice
  • ~2 Tbsp olive oil

Cilantro Lime Crema Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cilantro
  • 1/2 of a jalapeno, deseeded
  • 1 clove garlic
  • half a cup of greek yogurt (~1 prepackaged cup)
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • (optional) mayo
  • (optional) sour cream

Instructions

  1. Add cilantro, jalapeno, garlic, yogurt, honey, salt, olive oil to a blender, twister jar is recommended
  2. optionally add mayo and/or sour cream to make creamier
  3. blend, add liquid if blender can't start
