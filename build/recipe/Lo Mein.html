Ingredients

  • Lo Mein Noodles (or spaghetti)
  • Pork (Loin, cut to strips)

Instructions

  1. Cook Pasta, drain, set aside
  2. Heat oil in wok, add pork strips, get color on strips; remove pork strips
  3. Add ginger and garlic to oil. Cook for a bit. Add carrot, yellow onion, peppers, cook
  4. Add back in pork strips, add sesame oil, add scallions, add sugar and soy sauce
  5. drop in noodles, mix and serve
