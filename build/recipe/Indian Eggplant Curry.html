Indian Eggplant Curry Indian Simple

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggplants
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • cilantro

Instructions

  1. Poke holes into eggplants, wrape in foil, put into 400F oven for about 90 minutes
  2. In a pot or dutch over, heat ghee and add cardamon pods, cumin seeds, green peppercorn (or black), fresh diced chilis.
  3. Add diced onion and salt, cook for a few minutes
  4. Add ginger garlic paste, cook one minute
  5. Add 2 cans diced tomatoes. Cook down for 10 minutes.
  6. Scoup out eggplant meat, discard skins. Add meat to tomato sauce
  7. Add chopped cilantro
