Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • shredded cheese
  • pizza seasoning
  • tortilla
  • sauce for dipping (Crushed Tomatoes, ketchup, ranch, pizza sauce)

Instructions

  1. Sandwich pepperoni and cheese between two quesadillas. You want to use more cheese that a normal pizza topping. Optionally, add pizza seasoning
  2. Fry on a skillet and cut into dunkable strips
