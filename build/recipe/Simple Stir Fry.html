Ingredients

  • protein (chicken, beef, pork, shrimp, etc)
  • white rice
  • cabbage
  • spring onions
  • carrots
  • zuchini
  • broccoli
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • soy sauce
  • stir fry sauce

Instructions

  1. Make more than enough rice
  2. Fry protein in wok, remove and let sit
  3. Fry hardest vegetables in wok (carrots, onions)
  4. Add ginger, garlic next
  5. add soft vegetables (cabbage, zuchini, brocoli
  6. season with soy sauce
  7. Dice and reintroduce protein
  8. Serve with stir fry sauce on the side
