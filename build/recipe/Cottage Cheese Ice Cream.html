Cottage Cheese Ice Cream Dessert
Share Recipe 📧
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2Mr9GDb30zk 🔗

Ingredients

  • cottage cheese (full fat)
  • honey (optional)
  • peanut butter (optional)

Instructions

  1. blend all ingredients, put into tupperware and freeze
  2. Take out of freezer, pop into microwave and enjoy!
