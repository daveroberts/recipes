Chocolate Mousse Dessert

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Semi sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Add 2 cups ricotta cheese to food processor. Process until smooth
  2. Add 3 Tbsp sugar. Pinch of salt
  3. Melt 4oz semi sweet chocolate chips. Add to food processor
