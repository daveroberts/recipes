naan dough

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup avocado oil
  • 1/4 cup plain yoghurt
  • 2 3/4 cups ap flour
  • 3/4 tsp salt

garlic butter

Ingredients

  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 3/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Add water, sugar and yeast to mixing bowl, light mix, add oil, egg and yogurt, whisk together
  2. Lay flour on table, make well in center, pour wet into middle, knead the dough, add more flour if too sticky
  3. Form into ball, place into bowl, cover with towel, leave somewhere warm to rise
  4. Cut dough into eight pieces, roll into flat circles
  5. Heat cast iron, place dough onto dry pan, cook for about one minute, see bubbles form, flip, another minute of heat
  6. Brush with garlic butter and add flaky salt
