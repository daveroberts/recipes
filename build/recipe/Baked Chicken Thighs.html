Baked Chicken Thighs
Note: Family loved it!
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fks2SyKc0gs üîó

Marinade

Ingredients

  • 3-4 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 4 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp W sauce
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 5 Tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 3 Tbsp minced garlic (or more)
  • black pepper 1/2 tsp
  • 2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Marinade chicken thighs overnight or longer
  2. Add to baking pan and bake at 400 for 35 minutes
  3. Put under broiler for 5 minutes
