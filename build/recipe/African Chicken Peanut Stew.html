African Chicken Peanut Stew Slow Cooker

Ingredients

  • Chicken (Thighs or wings or drums or all)
  • 1-2 onions, sliced
  • 3 sweet potatoes, cubed
  • Tomatoes, 1 can cruashed or whole ~15 oz
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • ginger (3 inches, minced)
  • garlic (1 small bulb, minced)
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup peanuts
  • Kale (or any additional veggies)
  • 1 Tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp cayenne
  • chopped cilantro (optional)

Instructions

  1. brown chicken in air fryer
  2. Saute onions, then add ginger and garlic
  3. Add tomatoes, sweet potatoes, chicken broth, peanut butter, peanuts and spices. Stew for ~1 hour
  4. Add chicken and let it finish, maybe 30 minutes
  5. Put out chicken and de-bone / shred
  6. immersion blender on rest of stew, add lots of salt and pepper
  7. Add cilentra and chicken, let cook for 10 more minutes and serve
