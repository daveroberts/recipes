Turkey Melt Simple

Ingredients

  • Sliced Turkey
  • Flatbread
  • Lettuce
  • Salad Dressing (Honey Mustard)
  • Sriracha
  • Shredded Cheese (cheddar)

Instructions

  1. Finely chop lettuce. Add to bowl and mix with salad dressing
  2. Place cheese on flatbread. Place turkey on cheese. Place lettuce on turkey
  3. Fold in half and grill/press
