Ingredients

  • 6 32oz deli containers for dough, sauce
  • 500g flour
  • 100g vital wheat gluten
  • 10g salt
  • 5g instant yeast
  • 1.75 cups water

Instructions

  1. Put flour, wheat gluten, salt and yeast into a large food processor or bowl, blend/mix for a bit to mix
  2. Add cool water to blender/bowl slowly
  3. form into a ball, divide into five parts
  4. spray five containers with oil, ball dough and place into the container, add oil spray on top, place four intothe fridge for up to five days
  5. extra dough can be stored in the freezer. Spread as a pancake and put into a ziplock first
  6. Let one dough container rise in the container on the countertop

Pizza Sauce

Ingredients

  • 28oz Crushed Tomatos (Cento preferred)
  • 1.5g Garlic Powder
  • 6g salt

Instructions

  1. open crushed tomatoes, add garlic powder and salt to can, mix

Cheese

Ingredients

  • Block of part-skim low moisture mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Shred it yourself

Final Prep

Instructions

  1. Spray a 10 inch pan and your hands. Place ball of dough onto pan, press out evenly.
  2. Spread sauce onto pizza
  3. Add cheese to pizza
  4. Bake at 425F for 14 minutes
