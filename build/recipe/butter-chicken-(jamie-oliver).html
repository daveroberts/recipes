Instructions

Recipe

Instructions

  1. Put chicken on wooden skewers (use two to make flipping easier), heat under grill, keep turning every 15 minutes
  2. Add butter, spice mix and onions to pan. Heat until light brown
  3. Add ginger (1 Tbsp) and chillies (2), add garam masala (1/2 tsp) and tomato puree (3 Tbsp), add heavy cream (1/2 cup)
  4. Add water if curry is too thick, add honey (2 Tbsp) and fenugreek powder (1/2 Tbsp)
  5. Add cooked chicken and simmar for 10 minutes
