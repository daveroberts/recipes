Ingredients

  • Low carb tortilla
  • Light mozzarella cheese
  • turkey or chicken breast slices
  • pickled jalapenos or pickles

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients between two tortillas
  2. spray with oil
  3. air fry at 325F for 8 minutes
