Oatmeal Cookies Simple Dessert Breakfast
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4ZeK_iLauko 🔗

Ingredients

  • bananas
  • oatmeal
  • sugar free maple syrup (optional)
  • peanut butter (optional)

Instructions

  1. mix and mash all ingrediants
  2. put on parchment paper, smoosh cookies flat
  3. bake at 350 for 20 minutes
