Cheaper Beef and Broccoli

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp corn starch
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 spoonful minced garlic
  • rice
  • 1 pound lean beef
  • 2 pound bag of frozen broccoli

Instructions

  1. Start the rice
  2. microwave and drain the broccoli
  3. mix water, soy sauce, corn starch, sugar, ginger and garlic to make a sauce
  4. brown beef, move beef to the side and add broccoli to pan
  5. Add sauce, let it simmer
  6. Serve with rice
