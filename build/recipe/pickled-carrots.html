Pickled carrots
Share Recipe 📧
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NOgPQvUWfHk 🔗

Ingredients

  • 2-4 carrots, enough to fill non-metal jar
  • white vinegar, 1/2 cup
  • 1.5 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Cut carrot into strips, place into jar
  2. Fill jar 1/3 cup boiling water, 1/2 cup vinegar, put sugar and salt on top. Put on lid and shake
