Ingredients

  • meats: sausage, ground meat, cured meat (ham)
  • veggies - bell peppers, onions, celery, carrots
  • beans - red beans preferred
  • broth/stock - 8oz can
  • seasoning: cajun/creole
  • rice - long grain white rice preferred

Instructions

  1. Night before, Brown uncooked meat, saute veggies, soak beans in water
  2. Morning: Add meats, veggies, beans, broth and seasoning
  3. Before serving: cook rice
