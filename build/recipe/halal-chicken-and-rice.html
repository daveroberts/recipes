Marinade chicken

Ingredients

  • boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • olive oil
  • dried thyme
  • dried oregano
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • green cardamom powder
  • turmeric
  • paprika
  • allspice
  • black pepper
  • lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients. Use 1/2 in marinade and save the other half
  2. Mix and marinate until you loose patience

Chicken and rice

Ingredients

  • rice
  • (optional) stock

Instructions

  1. Fry chicken in some oil, remove chicken when cooked
  2. Add rest of seasoning mix, add some more oil, add rice, toast for a bit
  3. Add water or stock, bring to a boil, cover and simmer

Serve

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Dice lettuce and tomatoes
  2. Serve chicken, rice, lettuce, tomatoes and sauce
