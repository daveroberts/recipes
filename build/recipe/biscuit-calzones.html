Ingredients

  • Biscuits
  • Pizza sauce

Instructions

  1. flatten biscuits
  2. Add 1 spoonful pizza sauce
  3. Close with fork
  4. Brush with garlic butter
  5. Air fry, add cheese to top
  6. dunk in more pizza sauce
