Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons butter
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 1 egg
  • splash vanilla
  • 1 heaped Tablespoon baking powder
  • water or milk
  • Flour, bread or regular (almond flour won't work)

Instructions

  1. Barely melt butter into big pyrex jug (~30 seconds)
  2. Add sugar, whish with fork
  3. Add egg and a splash of vanilla, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 Tablespoon of baking powder, whisk together
  4. Add liquid to fill to 2 cup line
  5. Add flour, about 1 cup and a half, until consistency like pancakes, fold with liquid
