Burger 'n Bean Hot Dish Slow Cooker to try
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oXWr23_GQ3U üîó

Ingredients

  • Slow cooker liner
  • corn bread (optional)
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 16oz can barbecue beans
  • 1 11.5 oz can bean with bacon soup
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 Tbsp minced onion
  • shredded cheese (american preferred, cheddar OK)

Instructions

  1. Line slow cooker
  2. combine all ingredients except cheese
  3. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours
  4. Serve with corn bread
