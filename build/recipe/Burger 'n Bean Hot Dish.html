Burger 'n Bean Hot Dish Slow Cooker

Ingredients

  • Slow cooker liner
  • 1 16oz can barbecue beans (or regular beans plus barbecue sauce)
  • 1 11.5 oz can bean with bacon soup
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic salt (garlic powder is fine)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • shredded cheese (american preferred, cheddar OK)
  • corn bread (optional)

Instructions

  1. Line slow cooker
  2. combine all ingredients except ground beef and cheese
  3. Cover and cook on low for 4 hours
  4. Add beef one hour before done, break it up
  5. Top with cheese and serve with corn bread
