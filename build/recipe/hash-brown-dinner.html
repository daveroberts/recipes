Hash Brown dinner Cheap to try
Share Recipe 📧
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yk0EWFU5qOI 🔗

Ingredients

  • Hash Browns w/ Onions & Peppers
  • 12 eggs
  • bacon pieces
  • shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. cook hash browns in oil, add salt and pepper and seasoning, don't over-fry
  2. mix 12 eggs in a bowl
  3. oil a casserole dish, add potatoes, bacon and eggs in layers
  4. Bake at 375F until eggs are firm
