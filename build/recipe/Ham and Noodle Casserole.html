Ham and Noodle Casserole Slow Cooker to try
Share Recipe 📧
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oXWr23_GQ3U 🔗

Ingredients

  • Instant Pot as a slow cooker
  • 1 cup noodles
  • 1 cup cubed cooked ham
  • 1 10oz can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 Tbsp chopped pimiento
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped green pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles until barely tender, drain and toss with oil
  2. Add everything to the instant pot
  3. cook on low for four hours
