Ingredients

  • Instant Pot if single serving, slow cooker if double
  • 1 (2) cup noodles
  • 1 (2) cup cubed cooked ham
  • 1 (2) 10oz can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (2) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 (2) Tbsp chopped pimiento
  • 1/2 cup (1 cup) grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 (2) chopped green pepper
  • corn bread (optional)

Instructions

  1. If using a slow cooker, add liner
  2. cook on low for four hours
