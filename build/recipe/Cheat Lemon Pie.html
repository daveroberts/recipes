Cheat Lemon Pie Dessert
Share Recipe 📧
Note: Makes two pies, make sure you have enough room in your freezer
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p0W9GuCHMJg 🔗

Ingredients

  • 2x Pre-made graham cracker crust (or any crust)
  • 1 carton Cool Whip
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can lemonade concentrate, thawed

Instructions

  1. Combine ingredients into bowl, mix until combined
  2. Pour into pie crusts and freeze
