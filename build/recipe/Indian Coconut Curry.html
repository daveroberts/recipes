Ingredients

  • Butternut squash (or yams or carrots)
  • Coconut milk
  • chopped mushrooms (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet. Heat coconut oil. Add corriander, fenugreek, cinnamon sticks, mustard seeds, dried chilis. Heat until seeds start to pop.
  2. Add onions. Heat until brown. Add ginger garlic paste. Heat for another minute.
  3. Add chopped butternut squash. Cook for a few minutes
  4. Add coconut milk. Add turmeric
  5. Add mushrooms, add lime juice
  6. Cover and cook for a few more minutes
  7. Blend
